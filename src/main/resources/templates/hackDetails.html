Hack an Idea

Why have a hack day?

The idea of a “Hack Day” is to give all techies (Product and Engineering) break from their day-to-day planned work and create an environment of innovation and collaboration, by writing code.
Collaboration will be achieved by inviting all departments to submit ideas and physically sit with the techies to code their ask.

What is the hack?

This track is meant to create new ideas outside our core product. These ideas can be anything under the sun as long as they are smart and whacky and add value to Snapdeal in some form. This would be a team sport, with a team size of 2-6.

Rules

  • The submission of an entry will be accepted only using “What the Hack”.
  • An entry can be submitted by anyone in Snapdeal. But participation of the “sponsor” is mandatory for the hack e.g. if Payal from supply chain submits a feature, she will have to pair with 1 engineer, 1 QA, 1 Product Manager to execute this on the hack day.
  • The scope of any entry i.e. Idea, Feature or bug should be such that it can be prototyped or fixed in 12 hours i.e. by the EOD. It is okay to continue work later as well, but if it cannot be prototyped by EOD, it will not qualify for the prize.
  • The creation of the teams would be done on a voluntary basis using the took “What the Hack”. Sponsors of the ideas should talk to others to form the team.

Food & Stuff

  • Breakfast will be served till 9.30 AM in the cafeteria and we’ll kickstart the event.
  • We will reserve 2 floors in Tower A of ASF Center for the Hack day.
  • Hackers can choose anyplace to sit and collaborate and start hacking
  • Lunch (Pizza) will be served on the 2 Hack floors at 12. 30 PM for all the hackers.
  • At 9 PM we will officially end the hack day with dinner in the cafeteria.

Prizes

  • There will be scouts thought the day, who will look at different project and select some winners. The prizes will be announced on Friday in the Town Hall.
  • 3 Idea winners (Max 4 members).
  • 5 Bugs/Feature winners
